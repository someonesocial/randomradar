What is Random Radar?

Random Radar is a web discovery tool that finds and showcases content from newly discovered websites that haven't been indexed by major search engines yet. We scan certificate transparency logs, monitor newly registered domains, and explore the web's hidden corners to bring you fresh, unique content.
